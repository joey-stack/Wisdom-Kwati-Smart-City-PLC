# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Nigerian and Diaspora property buyers, families, and real estate investors looking for modern, smart, and secure housing in premium estate developments.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Wisdom Kwati Smart City residential estates and property portfolios across Nigeria, build client trust, and drive high-quality leads for properties and land sales.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, luxury-heritage editorial. Sophisticated, restrained, and expert-led. Evokes confidence, quiet luxury, and long-term security.

## Anti-references
- SaaS template clichés: glassmorphism, identical icon/card grids, tiny uppercase kickers/eyebrows above every section, or arbitrary numbered markers (01, 02).
- AI slop tells: bland/flat grays, over-saturated cream-neutral parchment background ramps, or overstimulating animations.

## Design Principles
- **Quiet Luxury & Restraint**: Focus on large, high-quality photography, generous whitespace, and minimal decoration. Let the properties speak for themselves.
- **Editorial Typography**: Pair clean sans-serif body copy with high-contrast, elegant serif headings to project a heritage, premium feel.
- **Structured Simplicity**: Avoid busy grids. Organize layout blocks into distinct, rhythmic, and spacious visual narrative steps.
- **Clear Information Density**: Present property data, floor plans, and amenities with high precision, clarity, and readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliant contrast ratios for all text elements.
- Clean typography hierarchy for readability.
- Support for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`) across page transitions and smooth scrolling.
