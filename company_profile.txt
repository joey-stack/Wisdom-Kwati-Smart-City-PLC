At the core of the Vision is the integration of intelligent infrastructure. 
The ultimate goal is to improve the overall quality of life for residents, a focus on well-being which includes healthcare, recreational facilities, and public spaces for social interaction.

WISDOM KWATI SMART CITY'S mission includes an urban landscape that harnesses technology and innovation to create a sustainable, resilient and inclusive community. It is a testament to the potential of urban planning in addressing the challenges of the 21st century, while enhancing the well-being of the people and the health of its clients. This is achieved by embracing smart solutions, sustainability and inclusivity. Wisdom Kwati aims to set a new standard for future cities in Africa.

WISDOM KWATI SMART CITY is one of the subsidiaries under Wisdom Kwati Group. Our desire is to bring smart homes to every single city in Africa while providing value on investment of each one of our client. 

WISDOM KWATI SMART CITY has a team of professionals with diverse backgrounds and a collective experience of construction and innovations over the years.   Our deep-seated passion is to deliver world class products and be a sure-delight to our clients. The company is privately owned.

Wisdom E. KWATI  (Chairman)
Wisdom Kwati is a name synonymous with success and leadership in the business world. As the founder, Chairman, and CEO of the Wisdom Kwati Group of companies and Karabow Group of companies, he has established himself as a trailblazer in the Nigerian business landscape. With multi-billion Naira conglomerates spanning various industries, including finance, real estate, agriculture, mining, education, technology, manufacturing, aviation, and banking, Wisdom Kwati has demonstrated exceptional entrepreneurial prowess and strategic acumen. 
Under his leadership, the Wisdom Kwati Group has become a formidable force, known for its innovation, integrity, and commitment to excellence.

In addition to his role as a business magnate, Wisdom Kwati holds several key positions, including Chairman and CEO of Kwati Airline, a Sierra Leone-based airline, Kwati investment ltd, Tanimala investment ltd and a host of other local and international companies. He is also the founder of the proposed Wisdom Kwati University in Demsa, Adamawa State and proposed Karabow Open University Abuja. He has also established partnerships with both government and private sectors and serves as the Chairman and CEO of Karabow Private Ltd Singapore, Wisdom Kwati Smart City LLC USA, among his many other international companies and businesses.

Beyond his remarkable achievements in the business world, Wisdom Kwati is recognized for his exceptional qualities as a leader. He is widely respected for his integrity, ethical principles, and strategic vision. He champions transparency, accountability, and responsible corporate governance, setting an exemplary standard for others to follow.

A vocal advocate for sustainable development, Wisdom Kwati has made environmental stewardship a cornerstone of his business philosophy. He promotes eco-friendly practices within his companies and actively encourages others to embrace sustainability. His dedication to environmental responsibility has earned him widespread recognition and praise, solidifying his reputation as a leader committed to the well-being of future generations.

In addition to his professional endeavors, Wisdom Kwati is deeply committed to philanthropy and social responsibility. Through the Wisdom Kwati Foundation, he has dedicated substantial resources to various causes, including food and water security, education, healthcare, and poverty alleviation. His initiatives, such as the establishment of boreholes across communities in Adamawa to provide clean drinking water and the Wisdom Kwati Scholarship Program, have transformed countless lives, providing opportunities for underprivileged individuals to thrive.

Recognized for his extraordinary achievements and contributions, Wisdom Kwati has received numerous accolades and honors, including prestigious business awards such as the 'Most Respected CEO in Real Estate.' His influence transcends borders, and he is widely regarded as a role model for aspiring entrepreneurs and philanthropists alike.

Wisdom Kwati's life and work are a testament to the power of vision, perseverance, and a commitment to making a positive impact. His business empire, built on integrity and ethical principles, and his philanthropic endeavors have undoubtedly made him a blessing to this generation and an inspiration for generations to come.
